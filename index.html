About Us

Welcome to Sintham Tiffen Center! At Sintham Tiffen Center, we believe that dining is more than just a meal—it’s an experience. Nestled in the heart of Salem, our restaurant is dedicated to bringing you delicious, SouthIndian-cuisine dishes crafted with passion and care.

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple dream: to create a place where friends and family could come together to enjoy great food and make lasting memories. With a love for SouthIndian-cuisine, we set out to open Sintham Tiffen Center with the goal of offering Salem a dining experience that is both memorable and meaningful.

Our Philosophy

At Sintham Tiffen Center, we are committed using locally-sourced ingredients. Each dish is prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients and a touch of creativity, ensuring that every bite is a delight.
